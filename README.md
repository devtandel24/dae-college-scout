# 🎓 College Scout
### Build your own AI agent that finds your best-fit colleges, trade schools, or whatever comes next after high school.

**Built for:** CT Tech Week 2026 · Synchrony Skills Academy × DAE · Beyond the Chatbot

---

## What you're about to do

In the next 45 minutes, you're going to build an AI agent. Not a chatbot. An **agent** — something that has memory, uses real tools, makes decisions, and gets actual work done in the world.

Your agent will:
- 🔍 **Research** colleges, trade programs, or pathways that fit YOU specifically (not generic recommendations)
- 📊 **Generate** a personalized Scout Report with your name on it
- ✉️ **Draft emails** to your top 5 picks, ready for you to send
- 📅 **Add deadlines** to your calendar with reminders 2 weeks before each one
- 📁 **Save everything** to your Google Drive in a folder built just for you

And it'll keep working for you after the workshop ends.

---

## Who built this & why

The DAE Agentic AI team built this to give you a real taste of what AI agents can do — and how the skill of *directing* an AI is more important than just using one. The same pattern you'll learn today (give the agent memory, give it tools, give it a clear job, watch it execute) is what powers every serious AI agent in the world right now.
