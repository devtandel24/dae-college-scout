# Workshop: Build Your College Scout
### CT Tech Week 2026 · Beyond the Chatbot · Stamford

Welcome. You're about to build an AI agent that finds your best-fit colleges, trade schools, or post-high-school pathway — using your real profile, doing real research, producing a real report with your name on it.

This isn't a chatbot using AI. This is **you directing an agent**.

---

## What you'll have built by the end of today

A working AI agent in your Claude account that:

- ✅ Knows you (your profile lives in its memory)
- ✅ Researches (it searches the real web — College Scorecard, college sites, scholarship databases)
- ✅ Makes decisions (it ranks schools/programs by fit to YOU specifically)
- ✅ Produces output (a personalized Scout Report you can share)
- ✅ Takes action (drafts emails, creates Drive folders, adds calendar deadlines)
- ✅ Persists (stays in your account, gets smarter about you over time)

---

## How to start

Follow `SETUP.md` step by step. Don't skip ahead — each step builds on the last.

If you get stuck, raise your hand. Your facilitator is in the room.
